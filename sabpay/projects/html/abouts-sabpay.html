About Us

Our goal is to make device indepedent payments so easy, safe and universally accepted that people never feel the need to carry cash or cards again. We believe India is at the cusp of a new revolution, which will change the way we manage our money on the go. We see ourselves facilitating this change, through technology and dogged customer centricity.
